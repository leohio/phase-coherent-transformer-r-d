## 2. Key findings

1. **PCT (csg) is the unique winner on content-driven algorithmic tasks**: holds even after 1.41× param compensation (0.15 points above real_screen 0.70).
2. **semi-PCT (cscr) is close to PCT** (0.833 vs 0.854, Δ=0.021, within seed std): screening's hard threshold is effective for algorithmic relational retrieval.
3. **real_screen is 1st on the real side** (0.698; real_sm/sig at 0.15-0.18 are far behind): the Phase 8/10/11 "real_screen is strong on phase/algorithmic tasks" pattern continues.
4. **Vanilla softmax (real and complex) all collapse on LRA-ListOps**: the row-norm constraint cannot solve long-range algorithmic dependency.
